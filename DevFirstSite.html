My thoughts

In Auriea Harvey and the Future of Digital Sculpture, the artist speaks about her experience learning technology and how her work has developed alongside the development of technology throughout the years. I thought it was really interesting how she used the different technologies to serve her purpose in the way that they could during the time. Harvey found a way to be creative and make art from the now seemingly primative web1 to now creating 3d art as the technical world emerges into web3. I found it inspiring how she not only used her work to create beatiful things but she also created meaningful pieces that represented her black identity in a field where you don't see many people like her and me. Seeing Harvey's story is a good way for me to frame my mindset for this class to not feel limited by the technology.
